Pluto - No Longer a Planet

Uh-oh! Pluto is no longer considered a planet in our solar system. In 2006, the International Astronomical Union (IAU) reclassified Pluto as a "dwarf planet."

Pluto is now part of the Kuiper Belt, a region of the solar system beyond Neptune that is populated with small icy bodies.

While Pluto may not be a planet, it remains an interesting celestial object that continues to be studied by astronomers and space enthusiasts.
